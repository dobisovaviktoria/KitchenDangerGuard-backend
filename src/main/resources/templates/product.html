What is KDG?

Kitchen Danger Guard is a safety device designed to prevent kitchen fires by alerting the users if they leave the stove on. When the product detects unattended stove activity by monitoring the motion and heat in the nearby area, the system sends a notification alerting the user to the potential danger. If the danger persists, the device triggers an alarm and in a dire situation calls the fire brigade. The product combines advanced technology with user-friendly functionality for better use.
